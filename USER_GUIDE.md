# Segadty POS - User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to Segadty POS! This system helps you manage sales, inventory, and reports for your rug store.

### 1. Login
*   Navigate to the homepage.
*   Enter your username and password.
*   **Note**: For this demo version, any username/password combination will work after a short delay.

### 2. Point of Sale (POS)
*   **Add Products**: Click on any product card in the grid to add it to the cart.
*   **Search/Filter**: Use the search bar or category buttons above the grid to find specific rugs.
*   **Manage Cart**:
    *   Use `+` and `-` in the sidebar to change quantities.
    *   Click the trash icon to remove an item.
    *   "Empty" clears the entire cart.
*   **Checkout**:
    *   Click "Complete Order" (إتمام الطلب).
    *   Fill in the customer details (Name and Phone are required).
    *   Select "Individual" or "Company".
    *   Click "Confirm" to generate the invoice.
    *   You can then **Print** or **Download PDF**.

### 3. Inventory Management
*   Go to the **Inventory** tab from the sidebar.
*   View all products, their cost/selling prices, and current stock.
*   Rows are highlighted based on stock status:
    *   **Green**: Available.
    *   **Orange**: Low Stock (needs reordering).
    *   **Red**: Out of Stock.
*   Use the "New Product" button to add items (Interface mock).

### 4. Reports
*   Go to the **Reports** tab.
*   View daily sales summaries, top-selling products, and simple charts.
*   Use "Export Excel" to download a report (mock functionality).

### 5. Settings
*   Check the system status to ensure the app is connected to the database and Google Sheets.
*   Use "Force Sync" if you believe the stock numbers are outdated.
